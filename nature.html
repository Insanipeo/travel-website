Reconnect with Nature

Have you noticed how most of the major attractions are tall, man-made structures? Believe me, I adore the city for what it is; but, nature delivers what the city cannot. I believe that the hustle and bustle of New York City is unparalleled in its criteria, but nature is calming; it's like reconnecting with oneself. If you recall all of the benefits of travel that I outlined on the "Why Travel?" page, I believe those benefits come from visiting in nature rather than in cities. Though it is subjective, the city may be calming and reducing stress for others. Anyway, I cannot adequately convey the essence of nature in words, but I can show you photographs, a thousand words, of the contrast between city and nature.

Versus.

I want you to keep this comparison in mind while I show you places I wish to visit, whether it's the city, historic structures, or simply nature. Then we shall conduct a survey on the million dollar question, "Nature or City?".
