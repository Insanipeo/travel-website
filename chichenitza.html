Ancient Wonder of the World

Prospect 1: Chichen Itza, a marvel of Mayan civilization, remains one of the most impressive ancient landmarks in the world. It is one of the New 7 Wonders of the World; the ruins are located in the northern half of Mexico's Yucatan Peninsula, where the Mayan people, numbering in the tens of thousands, formerly thrived.
